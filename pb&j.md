*Recipe

Step 1. Toast bread

Step 2. Add Peanut Butter to one slice

Step 3. Add Jelly on the other slice (You can also use apples!)

Step 4. Set the slice of bread with PB (PB side down) on the slice of bread with jelly (or apples!)
